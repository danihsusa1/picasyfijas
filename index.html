Guidelines

  • The goal is to guess the four non-repetitive digit number
  • On every attempt, you write your guess and hit "Send"
  • You are going to get clues that will help you guessig the correct number
  • Picas are the number of digits from your guess that do are on the secret number, but on a different position
  • Fijas are the number of digits from your guess that are already in the right position
